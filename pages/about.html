About Hindalco Industries Limited

Hindalco Industries Limited is the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a formidable US$26 billion metals powerhouse. Renowned as an industry leader in aluminium and copper, Hindalco has solidified its global presence.

In a landmark move, Hindalco acquired Aleris Corporation in April 2020 through its subsidiary, Novelis Inc. This strategic move has positioned the company as the world’s largest flat-rolled products player and a leading recycler of aluminium.

The state-of-the-art copper facility at Hindalco comprises a world-class copper smelter and a fertiliser plant, complemented by a captive jetty. This copper smelter stands as one of Asia's largest custom smelters at a single location.

Operating across India, Hindalco's aluminium units cover a comprehensive range of operations—from bauxite mining, alumina refining, coal mining, captive power plants, and aluminium smelting to downstream rolling, extrusions, and foils. Today, Hindalco stands among the global aluminium majors, boasting integration and a presence in 9 countries outside India.

The Birla Copper unit, a significant part of Hindalco, produces copper cathodes and continuous cast copper rods. It also generates valuable by-products, including gold, silver, and DAP fertilisers, making it India’s largest private producer of gold.

Recognized as a Star Trading House in India, Hindalco's aluminium is accepted for delivery under the High-Grade Aluminium Contract on the London Metal Exchange (LME). Additionally, its copper quality is registered on the LME with Grade A accreditation.
